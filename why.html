A number of startups have emerged over the past few years to use artificial intelligence (AI) to automate analysis of medical images such as Caption Health, PathAI, Paige and Zebra Medical Vision. There are several reasons why health tech is thriving:

1. Advancements in technology: With the increasing advancements in technology, it has become possible to develop more advanced and sophisticated medical devices and software systems that can improve the diagnosis, treatment, and management of various health conditions.

2. Increased demand for healthcare services: The global population is growing and aging, which has led to an increase in the demand for healthcare services. Health tech offers a way to address this demand by providing efficient and effective healthcare solutions.

3. Need for cost-effective healthcare: With rising healthcare costs, there is a need for more cost-effective healthcare solutions. Health tech offers a way to achieve this through remote monitoring, telemedicine, and other digital health solutions.

4. Regulatory support: Governments and regulatory agencies have recognized the potential of health tech to improve healthcare outcomes and have provided support in terms of funding, incentives, and regulatory frameworks to promote innovation in this field.

Above all, the convergence of technology, demand for healthcare services, cost-effectiveness, and regulatory support has created a fertile environment for health tech to thrive.
